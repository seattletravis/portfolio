Note: Please don't play this on a tablet or smartphone.
Written for the web browser so that it is easily accessible but...
it is meant to be played on a computer with a mouse and keyboard.

TILT3D by Travis Lamberte was written in Vanilla-JS, & HTML using Three-JS,
and Cannon-ES for the physics engine.
This project also uses Vite, Node, GSAP, Tailwind among other modules.
You can clone the single and multiplyer versions of this game at
https://github.com/seattletravis/Tilt3dOnePlayer.git
https://github.com/seattletravis/Tilt3dMultiPlayer.git
Select a player mode in the menu to get started.

ONE PLAYER SELECTED - PRESS "START GAME"

Goal:
Go 10 rounds (10 tiles) to score as many points as possible.
Max Score 1000 Points (100 Points per Tile)

Game Play:
Mouse click to grab a tile & release the mouse to drop the tile.
Releasing a tile over your score bubble will tally your points.
Be Careful! If the round top-block goes too far off center…
Ka-bloo-ee! The tower explodes, the game ends, and you lose.
You can move the camera position by hovering over the hover-controls
(Keyboard keys enabled “a, s, d, w” Left, Down, Right, Up & “i, o, r” In, Out, Reset.)

Scoring:
Scores for each turn can be anywhere between 0-100 points.
The farther the top-block is from center the less points the
current tile is worth, when it's dropped in the score bubble.
(use the “Maximum Score” readout to see what your score will be when you drop your tile)
TIP: You can nudge the tower and click on any of the tiles as much as you want.
